About Gamers Hall

Gamers Hall is a new organization for the 2017/2018 academic school year. Gamers Hall was born out of the burning passion of two student organizations with over 100 members each that decided to work together to promote the gaming initiative on campus. This Summer/Fall, we're bringing you the League of Legends High School Invitational! With over $10,000 in scholarships and prizes available, we're proud to say this event will knock your socks off! You can find more information about Gamers Hall here. You can find details about the IUPUI League of Legends High School Invitational below!

Event Details

The IUPUI League of Legends High School Invitational will start with a double elimination bracket on the week of February 4th. A round will occur each week with a specified time each team will play. The players are able to reschedule their matches if they are unavailable at the specified time, but each match must take place during its designated week. After the winners bracket reaches 4 teams and the losers bracket reaches 2 teams, all official matches between teams will stop until September 9th. The matches will then be played in-person on September 9th. Only high school students can sign up and a faculty/staff contact is required. You can find more detailed rules here. GL HF!
